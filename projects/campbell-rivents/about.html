What is Campbell Rivents?

Campbell Rivents strives to bring teens together through our services. Youth in Campbell river don't have very much to do. If you aren’t in to driving around with friends and listening to music, sitting in a parking lot and making jokes, or long walks in the woods, you’re out of luck for the most part. Teens are turning to worse things to do to spend their time doing.

Because of this, they’re turning to things like drugs/drinking/vaping/etc. Doing things like this early on in life can have serious health effects. Teens are stuck in their friend groups doing the same old things, so how can we change this rut that teens find themselves to be in?

Here is our solution.


We are working on an app (right now it’s just a website mockup) that anyone can use to let others know of events going on in town. It would be something similar to Facebook's events page, but this generation is moving away from Facebook. We’re offering an innovation.

With this, teens can register an account, and create events and make them public to others in their area. If an event is happening near you, a push notification will be sent to your phone and alert you to the event. Clicking the notification will provide more info on the event such as the time/place/description of the event.
