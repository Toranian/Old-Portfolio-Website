Who am I?

My name is Isaac Morrow, and I’m a front-end web developer. I’m currently enrolled in Timberline Secondary School and in grade 11. I have a passion for the outdoors, philosophy, and especially programming. At school, I excel at getting straight A’s and achieving the Principal's Honor Roll, while also understanding the subject matter. In March of 2019, I won the regional web development competition for skills Canada, after only 3 months since beginning my web development journey. I am excited to see how my skills will improve in later projects and creations.
